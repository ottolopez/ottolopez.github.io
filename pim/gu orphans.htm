Guatemala Orphans

Guatemala, a country of 18 million people, has 300,000 orphans.

Some are left at hospitals, doorsteps of churches or in the street.

The government rescues some from families who have abused the children or there is no family to care for them.

PiM has entered into a partnership with two orphanages listed below to care for these helpless children...

Mimi's House and House of Hope.
