Our Purpose...

Help the Indigenous People become spiritually mature that their churches,

with the help of the Holy Spirit,

 may be self-governing, self-propagating and self-reliant

 

 

 

 

Partners in Missions is...

~Governed by its membership and Board of Directors

~Approved by IRS as a nonprofit religious organization

All donations are fully tax deductible.

 

 

 

Our support comes entirely from...

 individuals and congregations

who share a common burden for reaching the Indigenous People.

We are not funded by any certain denomination

 

 

 

Areas of ministry include...

~Faith Promise Missions Conventions

~Providing Short-term missions opportunities

~Planting and constructing of churches

~Working with missionaries and their sending agencies

~Sponsorship program for students attending the 

D.S. Warner Christian School in Chepen, Peru

~Leadership Development
